About the Game

FC 25 is the latest football simulation game from EA Sports. Featuring realistic graphics, dynamic gameplay, and the most immersive football experience yet, FC 25 lets players build their dream teams and compete at the highest levels.

Gameplay Modes

  • Kick-Off: Quick matches for casual play.
  • Career Mode: Manage your team to glory.
  • Ultimate Team: Build and compete with custom squads.
  • Tournaments: Compete in league-style play or knockouts.

Winning Strategies

  • Possession Play: Control the ball to dominate matches.
  • Custom Tactics: Set up strategies to exploit opponent weaknesses.
  • Skill Moves: Use dribbling tricks to bypass defenders.

Tournament Rules

  • 1v1 matches with official EA Sports FC settings.
  • Each match lasts 6 minutes per half.
  • Knockout format with single elimination.

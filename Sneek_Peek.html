The most exciting stage of the tournament! Teams battle it out in a knockout format to claim the championship trophy.

Tournament Overview

The TEK-UP Esports Tournament follows a structured path to ensure fair and competitive gameplay. Here’s how it works:

  • Qualifiers: Open rounds to determine the best teams.
  • Group Stage: Teams are divided into groups to compete in round-robin matches.
  • Playoffs: Top teams proceed to the knockout stage leading to the finals.

Game Rules

Each game has its own set of rules tailored to the competitive format. Here’s a summary:

  • League of Legends: Best-of-3 matches with a draft phase for champions.
  • CS:GO: Standard competitive rules, with 30 rounds in a match.
  • Valorant: First to 13 rounds, with an overtime tie-breaker.

Guidelines

Participants are required to follow the official tournament guidelines:

  1. All players must register through the official platform.
  2. Sportsmanship is mandatory; toxic behavior will result in penalties.
  3. Matches will be monitored by referees to ensure fairness.
  4. Teams must adhere to scheduled match timings.
